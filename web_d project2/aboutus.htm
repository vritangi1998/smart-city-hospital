Clemency Hospital

Clemency Hospital was setup in 1993 based in New Delhi, India. It was founded by Mr. Ram Sharma in the name of his father Late Satyedev Sharma who died in the year 1989. Initially , the hospital was recognised for its special hand in cancer but as the time passed the hospital grew in other fields .In 2012,it was listed in the top 10 hospitals of India.

The hospital provides prosperous, peaceful and clean environment for the patients in order to increase their rate of improvement.1500 staffs,200 doctors work throughout the day for the patients as the hospital says "Take Care".
